HOW IT WORKS?

Using the power of QR Code and Photos, with Lost and Paws it helps to manage lost and found pets in the community. QR Code as pets ID, to easily store the info of our beloved pets in the system. Register your pets to our system to get a QR Code and put the Generated QR Code to your pet's collars.

In case your pet get lost it's information can easily find by scanning its QR Code,with that anyone finds your pet can contact you instantly. In case that your pet is not registered who ever find your pet can report to our system and submit a found report. Same with the lost pets, if the pet is not registered who ever find your pet can report to our system and submit a lost report.
